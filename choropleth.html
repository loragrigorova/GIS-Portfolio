SDG 5 Choropleth Map

An interactive map showcasing the percentage of parliamentary seats held by women in each country, in accordance with SDG target 5.5.1.

This map depicts one of the target indicators of the United Nations’ fifth Sustainable Development Goal: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. More specifically, the indicator I have depicted here is 5.5.1 (a): Proportion of seats held by women in national parliaments (United Nations Statistics Division, 2026). Through this indicator, we can measure the extent to which each country is working towards achieving Target 5.5, which aims to ensure women’s full and effective participation and equal opportunities for leadership at all levels of decision-making. The percentage of women’s representation in national parliaments is a useful metric for measuring progress toward Target 5.5, as it provides a direct and quantifiable overview of the degree to which women participate in and influence political leadership and decision-making.
